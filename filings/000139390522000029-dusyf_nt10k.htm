PART III - NARRATIVE

 

State below in reasonable detail the reasons why Form 10-K, 20-F, 11-K, 10-Q, 10-D, N-SAR, N-CSR, or the transition report or portion thereof could not be file within the prescribed time period.

 

Management of Duesenberg Technologies Inc. (the “Company”) was unable to obtain the business information necessary to complete the preparation of the Company’s audited consolidated financial statements for the year ended October 31, 2021, and the review of these financial statements by the Company’s auditors in time for filing. Such information is required in order to prepare a complete filing. As a result of this delay, the Company is unable to file its Annual Report on Form 10-K within the prescribed time period without unreasonable effort or expense. The Company expects to file within the extension period.
